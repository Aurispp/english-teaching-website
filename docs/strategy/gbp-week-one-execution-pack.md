## Week One Owner Checklist

This is the smallest sequence before publishing the first GBP update.

1. Record GBP baseline from owner UI.
   - Date/time.
   - Views.
   - Searches.
   - Website clicks.
   - Calls.
   - Messages.
   - Bookings if visible.
   - Top search terms if shown.

2. Check profile basics.
   - Website link points to `https://englishwithauris.com/`.
   - Phone/WhatsApp/contact options are correct.
   - Service area/local wording is true for Castelldefels, Gava and online.
   - Hours/availability do not imply a physical academy schedule if that is not true.

3. Add or confirm service entries.
   - Private English classes.
   - Online English classes.
   - English classes in Castelldefels and Gava.
   - English for professionals.
   - Conversation and confidence.
   - Business English for meetings, interviews and presentations.
   - Small-group English classes.
   - Cambridge/exam support only as secondary if still true.

4. Add or confirm one photo.
   - Preferred: friendly Auris photo or real online-class setup.
   - Alternative: Talk the Talk screenshot.
   - Avoid: logo-only, stock-like class photo, student-identifiable screenshot, fake review-star graphic.

5. Reply to visible reviews before or during Week 1.
   - Use short outcome-specific replies.
   - Reply in the language of the review where possible.
   - Do not keyword-stuff.

6. Publish only GBP Post 1.
   - Destination: homepage UTM.
   - Button: Learn more or Book, depending on owner UI.
   - Do not include the phone number in the post body.

7. Track every inquiry manually.
   - Ask: "Where did you find me?"
   - Note whether the lead is local/professional/price-shopping/referral/current-student-related.

## Decision Rule After 14 Days

Continue GBP proof posting if:

- GBP website clicks, calls or messages rise above baseline, or
- one qualified lead mentions Google/profile/reviews, or
- the homepage UTM appears in analytics/source notes, or
- profile search terms show relevant local/class intent.

Shift effort to profile cleanup/reviews before more posting if:

- profile actions do not move and the profile lacks photos/services/review replies, or
- inquiries mention price confusion from marketplace pages, or
- the only signals are low-price marketplace comparisons.

Keep LinkedIn as credibility-only if:

- it creates views/likes but no DMs, link clicks, Talk starts or professional inquiries after two posts.
