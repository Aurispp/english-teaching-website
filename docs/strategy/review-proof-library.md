## Core Proof Themes

Evidence:

- Andrea says classes helped her recover confidence and feel more secure in meetings with native speakers.
- Raquel says the biggest change after almost three years is confidence speaking.
- Silvia says she now feels comfortable having conversations without embarrassment or fear of mistakes.

Use:

- Homepage/local proof block.
- GBP post about confidence.
- Social posts about "speaking without apologizing."

Short copy options:

- `Students often mention the same thing: more confidence when they actually have to speak.`
- `The goal is not just more English. It is feeling able to use it when it matters.`

### Personal Adaptation

Evidence:

- Reviews mention adapting to level, needs, rhythm, weak points, and individualised sessions.

Use:

- Local page section answering "academy vs private classes."
- Trial-class CTA.

Short copy options:

- `Classes are adapted to the person in front of me, not a fixed academy script.`
- `We work on the habits that block your English, not just the next page of a textbook.`

### Professional Situations

Evidence:

- Andrea specifically mentions meetings with native speakers.
- Public profile copy and reviews support adult/professional use.

Use:

- Business page and LinkedIn posts.
- GBP service descriptions.

Short copy options:

- `Useful for meetings, interviews, presentations, client calls, and the moments where clear English matters.`
- `Professional English works best when practice feels close to your real work situations.`

### Enjoyable and Dynamic Classes

Evidence:

- Reviews mention friendly, funny, dynamic, entertaining, clear explanations, and practice-heavy classes.

Use:

- Social proof in posts.

Short copy options:

### Continuity Between Classes

Evidence:

- Andrea mentions new tools and relevant articles.
- Eli mentions videos, series, and books outside class.
- The portal provides flashcards, assignments, materials, class notes, games, and review.

Use:

- Premium price justification.
- Platform showcase.

Short copy options:

- `What comes up in class becomes something you can review, reuse, and bring back next time.`
- `The portal keeps vocabulary, materials, and practice from disappearing between classes.`

## Cycle 21 Review And Referral Request Rule

Cycle 21 created `referral-review-request-loop.md`.

Decision:

- Reviews should be requested through a small personal loop, not a bulk campaign.
- The ask should invite honest specifics from students with enough real experience, not only predicted-positive reviewers.
- Do not offer discounts, gifts, raffle entries, free classes or any other incentive for Google reviews.
- Do not ask for 5 stars, keywords, city names, or exact phrases.
- Do not combine review requests and referral asks by default.

Best review moments:

| Moment | Why It Works | Ask Type |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Student says they feel more confident speaking | Matches the strongest existing proof theme. | Review |
| Student uses English successfully in a meeting/interview/client call | Creates professional proof without generic business-English copy. | Review or referral |
| Student mentions that class follow-up helped | Makes the portal/follow-up value visible. | Review |
| Student naturally mentions a friend/colleague with the same problem | Warm referral context. | Referral |
| Former student returns or recommends Auris spontaneously | Shows durable trust. | Thank-you/referral |

Short safe ask:

`If the classes have helped and you ever feel like leaving an honest Google review, it would help me a lot. No pressure at all, and only write what feels true to your experience.`

Spanish:

`Si las clases te han ayudado y algun dia te apetece dejar una resena honesta en Google, me ayudaria mucho. Sin ninguna presion, y escribe solo lo que sea verdad para ti.`

Referral sentence students can repeat:

`Auris helps Spanish speakers who already know some English but freeze when they have to use it, especially in real conversation and work situations.`

Spanish:

`Auris ayuda a hispanohablantes que ya saben algo de ingles pero se bloquean cuando tienen que usarlo, sobre todo en conversacion real y situaciones de trabajo.`

Measurement:

```text
Students often mention confidence, but the work behind it is practical: real speaking practice, useful correction and review between classes.
```

Meeting Voice proof paragraph:

```text
In class, we practise the exact kind of moment you face, correct the Spanish-to-English patterns that slow you down, and keep useful phrases ready to review between classes. That is the kind of confidence students often mention: not perfect English, but feeling more able to use it when the real moment arrives.
```

Avoid:

- `5.0 / 16 reviews` in static copy until owner review truth confirms the count and reply status.
- `Business English for meetings` without a mechanism.
- Marketplace proof as a campaign trust layer.
- Review asks that request exact wording, keywords, service names or city names.
