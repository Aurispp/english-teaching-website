## Research Rules

- Do not equate virality with buying intent.
- Capture the exact language people use for pain, not just the topic.
- Distinguish learner interest from payer intent.
- Prefer patterns across several sources over one striking post.
- Before browsing, brainstorm where the evidence for the question is most likely to live.
- After browsing, record which sources were high-signal and which were low-signal.
- Every trend should become either:
  - a hypothesis,
  - a content test,
  - an offer test,
  - a platform/product idea,
  - or a reason not to act.
